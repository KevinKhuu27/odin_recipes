Vietnamese Summer Rolls

This is a recipe for preparing Vietnamese Summer Rolls. These rolls are great for parties and potluck as they are filling, easy to bring, and most importantly, enjoyable to eat.

Being part Vietnamese, I highly recommend trying this recipe with your family and friends. Anyone can try the recipe and is a great way to bond with others.

Ingredients Used

  • Rice Paper
  • Pork Belly
  • Basil
  • Vermicelli
  • Shrimp
  • Lettuce
  1. Boil the pork belly and shrimp under medium heat
  2. Boil the noodles in a seperate pot under medium heat
  3. Wash and prepare the lettuce and basil while waiting for everything to boil
  4. Once cooked, slice the shrimp into halves, and pork belly into slices
  5. Once ready, rinse the noodles under cold water
  6. Group the noodles into small handfulls/batches
  7. Split lettuce leafs into the desired roll length, prioritizing the stem
  8. Prepare plastic wrap for the desired roll length
  9. Dip rice paper into water for a few seconds and place onto a plate
  10. Place lettuce, shrimp, pork, basil, and noodles onto the rice paper, respectively
  11. Fold the rice paper up from the bottom to cover the filling
  12. Fold sides in and roll everything upwards, away from you
  13. Wrap the summer rolls using the plastic wrap
